Welcome to My MBA Blog!

Hi there! Thanks for stopping by. My name's Matt, and I'm a software engineer. I am also a part-time student at Carnegie Mellon University, working towards my Masters in Business Administration.

Something something about my background...

Currently, I work on the IT side of things at Carnegie Mellon University. As a member of a small development team, I build and maintain a variety of applications that enable faculty, staff, and students to connect to and use campus networks.

The motivations behind this blog are threefold:

  1. To chronicle my journey towards earning an MBA while working full-time;
  2. To provide an honest perspective of an employee of a university using his tuition benefits to earn a degree from said university (a perspective that was challenging to glean leading up to my enrollment in the program);
  3. To finally write a blog of some kind during a relatively significant life event, something that's been encouraged by family and friends (thanks, Mom).

My goal is to publish a post about once a week throughout the duration of the program. If you like what you see or have any questions, please reach out! I'm always looking to build out my network, and I'm happy to chat.
